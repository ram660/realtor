About Raja Allam

I, Raja Sesidhar Reddy Allam, was born and raised in India and immigrated to Vancouver in 2014. I came from a background with 7+ years as a customer service professional which brings me excellent organizational skills, detail-oriented skills, and exceptional interpersonal skills.

I attended Fairleigh Dickinson University and earned a Master's Degree in Computer Security & Forensics, Vancouver, BC. This has given me a unique insight into how to analyze the data accurately to provide valuable and unique information to the clients.

I entered the Real Estate industry since I have a great passion for real estate. I speak fluent English and Telugu. I am committed to providing exceptional and undivided service to my clients with my previous background and experience, I opt to never stop learning attitude towards every aspect of my life. I spend my free time hiking, playing outdoor sports, etc.
